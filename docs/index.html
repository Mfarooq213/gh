FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can I contact you?

If you are not yet a member go to the CONTACT US tab on the website at www.seekglobalnetwork.com. If you are already a member go to your virtual office select the CONTACT SUPPORT tab in your member’s menu.

How do I renew/ upgrade my membership?

Membership automatically renews each month. To Upgrade go to the UPGRADE ACCOUNT tab in your virtual office then choose the membership level that you wish to have.

What banner formats can be placed?

There are no technical restrictions on the banner sizes that can be placed. The most popular are 468x60, 728x90 and 125x125, but we recommend that you view the banner yourself before launch to see how well it fits on the screen.

How do I downgrade my membership?

Membership fees are non refundable. To downgrade your membership go to the UPGRADE ACCOUNT tab in your virtual office then choose the membership level that you wish to have. Those who wish to downgrade are advised to do so 2-4 days before automatic renewal date.

What is an ad packs and ad credits?

Adpacks are products where you can earn a reward of an average of .07 to 1 percent per day by viewing 10 ads. Ad credits are equivalent to money within the system to allow you ads to run on our platform. Each time an ad is viewed a credit is deducted from your ad credit balance.

How much are adpacks?

Each Adpack is $25. Ad packs give you the following ad credits: 10K banner ads 5K text ads 500 surfing credits
